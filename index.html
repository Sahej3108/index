Overview

30+ Years of Expertise

With over three decades of excellence, Takkar Polychem Pvt. Ltd. has become a trusted partner to leading multinational companies across automotive, electrical, consumer, medical, and wire & cable industries. Our legacy is built on an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and reliability - delivering high-performance compounds and masterbatches that meet diverse customer needs. Each product undergoes rigorous testing, continuous improvement, and process optimization, ensuring consistent performance and long-term partnerships.

Global Compliance Partner

As a compliance-first organization, we adhere to stringent national and international standards to ensure safety, reliability, and environmental responsibility in every product we manufacture. We produce 100% RoHS 3.0-compliant and phthalate-free compounds and masterbatches for leading global brands across industries.
Our ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and CE certifications stand as a testament to our dedication to quality, consistency, and customer trust.
